Real-world Math Problem

📌Your mother pays you 12 GHS per hour to babysit your sister on weekends. You offer your brother , Tachema, 10% of your weekend salary to cover Saturday night from 9 p.m. to 3 a.m.

Total hours in the weekend = 48 hrs

Payment for 1 hr = 12 GSH

GSH → Ghanian Shilling

Total hours Tachema cover → 9 a.m to 3 p.m = 6hrs
Total money earned = 12 * 48 = 576 GHS
Tachema's share
= 576 * 10/100
= 57.6 GHS
Money kept = 518.4 GHS

Market size for driverless cars

📌 What is the market size for driverless cars by 2028?

Total Cars Driverless Cars
Cars 1.4 Billion 30 Million
Average cost per car $46,000 $70,000
Market $56 Trillion $2.1 Trillion
CARG 32.3% 8.6%

Market size
= initial market * (1+CAGR)^n
CARG → Compound Annual Growth Rate
n → Difference in years
Market size in 2028
= 2.1T * (1+0.086)^3
= 2.7 Trillion
